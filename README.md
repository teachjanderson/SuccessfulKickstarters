# SuccessfulKickstarters
This analysis suggests criteria for a successful Kickstarter campaign based on launch date and goal amount

**Overview of the Project**
The goal of this project is to provide my client with two criteria to ensure a successful Kickstarter campaign in the area of performing arts. These are a preferred launch date and a suggested goal amount. Through analzying over 1000 successful and failed campaigns, ranging back nearly ten years and from less than $1000 to more than $50,000, it is possible to provide suggestions on these two key metrics.
